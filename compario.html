Compario

Comparator of strange units

About project

Concept of Compario is simple - Comparator of many values. We enter a value into search bar, choose unit (like currency, speed or square) and application shows approximate results. For example:

  • 3 000 000 000 $ is the same as Fortnite earned in 2018 or Poland spent on higher education
  • 390 km/h - The Peregrine Falcon speed
  • One more !

The greatest application I'm creating

Compario is the most extensive of all my current projects. List of all features:

  • Creating accounts and the ability to add data. Reputation and statistic of users,
  • Add CI/CD tool like Jenkins,
  • Open GraphQL API,
  • Dividing the application into microservices.
