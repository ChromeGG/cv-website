Compario

The comparator of fun and various units

About project

Concept of Compario is simple - Comparator of many values. We enter a value into the search bar, choose unit (like currency, weight, speed etc.) and the application shows approximate results. For example:

  • 3 000 000 000 $ is the same as Fortnite earned in 2018 or Poland spent on higher education
  • 3 600 kg - the mass of female African Elephant or tongue of a blue whale
  • 390 km/h - The Peregrine Falcon speed, the fastest animal in the world

Frontend

Front of application is created in Vue.js with Material Design Bootstrap. All are responsive of course.

Backend

There is no surprise - Spring Boot. But, there are a few interesting things. MongoDB is the main database. All repositories, services and controllers of units are generic. Compario API supports HATEOAS, soo the endpoints are RESTful.
